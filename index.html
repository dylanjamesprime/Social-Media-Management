FAQ

How do I know if it's right for me?

If you are a business owner or you have a personal brand and want to grow and sell on social media and you are willing to invest in a growth marketing strategy that has already been proven to work then book a call. Do not call if you are not looking to invest in yourself and your business.

Why should I have a personal brand?

If you have a skill, a trade, a service, or a good, that you can market and sell, then you should have a personal brand. Building your personal brand is about intentionally shaping how others see you to attract opportunities, build trust, and stand out. All of the content you watch on YouTube, consume on TikTok, Interact with on Instagram or Facebook, is the product of a personal brand.

Why should my business be on social media?

Over 5.2 billion people use social media globally as of late 2024/early 2025, representing over 60% of the world's population, with numbers still growing steadily. In the U.S., around 246-253 million people use it daily, covering about 73% of the nation's population, with Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook being the most popular platforms.

As of 2025, billions use social media for shopping, with around 5.24 billion users worldwide actively on platforms, and a significant portion buying goods and services. About 48% of users have bought directly on social media apps, while globally, over 72% research products there, with social commerce sales projected to hit $1.2 trillion globablly end of year.

If you truly want to sell to as many people as possible, then your business need to be on social media.

How time consuming is it?

Most of the workload is done for you by us. You just need to film. The time it takes you to film can very between plans. You can expect it to take 4-8 hours per month. Possibly more. This varies from person to person and may take longer if you are just starting out.

What kind of results can I expect to see?

Results will vary depending on your niche, target audience, and posting frequency. Posting frequently and consistently is the key to succes on social media. We will make a framework for you. You just need to adhere to it and film content that we script for you.

What is short form content?

Short form content is a video, 60 seconds or less, in 9:16 aspect ratio or vertical format. These consist of Instagram Reels, TikTok's, Youtube Shorts, etc.

What social media platforms do you work with?

Instagram, TikTok, Youtube, Facebook, Linkedin, and X (formerly Twitter). We prefer Instagram because, as of now, it's the best platform to grow and sell on social media for new and established business owners. Instagram and TikTok target a younger demographic, whereas Facebook and Linkedin target an older demographic. YouTube favors educational videos, whereas X favors polarizing videos.

How long does it take to see results?

You will start seeing results when we start posting videos. After completing the strategy call, researching, scripting, filming, and editing takes about 3 weeks and then the posting begins!
